# Civilization Sources

References for civilization founding/end dates, geographic regions, traits, scripts, and language codes.

## General

- Trigger, Bruce G. *Understanding Early Civilizations: A Comparative Study*. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2003.
  Comparative framework for ancient civilization traits, urban planning, writing systems.

- Stearns (2001), civilization entries.
  Founding/end dates, geographic extents, peak periods.

## Regional

- Twitchett and Fairbank (1978--2015). Chinese civilization continuity and dynastic transitions.
- Kulke and Rothermund (2016). South Asian civilizations: Indus Valley, Maurya, Gupta, Chola, Vijayanagara, Mughal.
- Connah, Graham. *African Civilizations*. 3rd ed. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2015.
  Ghana, Mali, Songhai, Great Zimbabwe, Kingdom of Kongo, Aksumite Empire, Kingdom of Kush.
- Coe (2013). Mesoamerican civilizations: Olmec, Maya, Aztec.
- D'Altroy, Terence N. *The Incas*. 2nd ed. Malden: Wiley-Blackwell, 2014.
  Inca Empire dates, traits, and extent.
- Higham, Charles. *The Civilization of Angkor*. London: Weidenfeld and Nicolson, 2001.
  Khmer Empire, Angkor period dating.

## Scripts and Language Codes

- Daniels, Peter T., and William Bright, eds. *The World's Writing Systems*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1996.
  Script identifications and classifications.
- ISO 639-3, SIL International. Language code assignments.
