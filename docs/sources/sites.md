# Site Sources

References for archaeological site occupation dates, discovery dates, locations, and civilizations.

## General Archaeology

- Renfrew, Colin, and Paul Bahn. *Archaeology: Theories, Methods, and Practice*. 8th ed. London: Thames and Hudson, 2020.
  Site dating methodology, excavation history conventions.

- Scarre, Chris, ed. *The Human Past: World Prehistory and the Development of Human Societies*. 4th ed. London: Thames and Hudson, 2018.
  Occupation dates and cultural associations for prehistoric and ancient sites.

## UNESCO Heritage

- UNESCO World Heritage Centre. *World Heritage List: Advisory Body Evaluations*. Paris: ICOMOS/IUCN, 1978--present.
  Site descriptions, dating, and significance assessments for: Gobekli Tepe, Catalhoyuk, Ur, Persepolis, Petra, Giza, Valley of the Kings, Carthage, Knossos, Mycenae, Pompeii, Mohenjo-daro, Ajanta Caves, Terracotta Army, Angkor Wat, Borobudur, Teotihuacan, Tikal, Chichen Itza, Machu Picchu, Great Zimbabwe, Lalibela, Stonehenge, Lascaux, Newgrange.

## Regional

- Schmidt, Klaus. *Gobekli Tepe: A Stone Age Sanctuary in South-Eastern Anatolia*. Berlin: ex oriente, 2012.
  Gobekli Tepe dating and interpretation.
- Hodder, Ian. *The Leopard's Tale: Revealing the Mysteries of Catalhoyuk*. London: Thames and Hudson, 2006.
  Catalhoyuk occupation dates and settlement structure.
- Woolley, C. Leonard. *Ur of the Chaldees*. Rev. ed. London: Herbert Press, 1982.
  Ur Royal Tombs, ziggurat dating.
