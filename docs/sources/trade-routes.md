# Trade Route Sources

References for trade route active periods, commodities, endpoints, and civilizations.

## General Trade History

- Curtin, Philip D. *Cross-Cultural Trade in World History*. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1984.
  Framework for trade network periodization; covers all 15 routes.

- Frankopan, Peter. *The Silk Roads: A New History of the World*. London: Bloomsbury, 2015.
  Silk Road, Maritime Silk Road, Incense Route, Spice Route dating and commodity lists.

## Silk Roads

- Liu, Xinru. *The Silk Road in World History*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2010.
  Silk Road start date (Zhang Qian mission ~119 BCE), commodity flows, civilizations involved.

## Indian Ocean and Maritime

- Beaujard, Philippe. *The Worlds of the Indian Ocean: A Global History*. 2 vols. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2019.
  Maritime Silk Road, Spice Route, Austronesian Maritime Network dating and commodity lists.

## Trans-Saharan

- Austen, Ralph A. *Trans-Saharan Africa in World History*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2010.
  Trans-Saharan trade start date (~300 CE with camel domestication), gold-salt exchange.

## European

- Dollinger, Philippe. *The German Hansa*. Translated by D. S. Ault and S. H. Steinberg. London: Macmillan, 1970.
  Hanseatic Trade Network dates and commodity lists.

## Atlantic

- Mintz, Sidney W. *Sweetness and Power: The Place of Sugar in Modern History*. New York: Penguin, 1985.
  Triangular Trade commodity flows and periodization.
