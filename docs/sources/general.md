# General References

Standard reference works consulted across all itihas data modules.

## World History Encyclopedias

- Stearns, Peter N., ed. *The Encyclopedia of World History*. 6th ed. Boston: Houghton Mifflin, 2001.
  Primary chronological reference for dates, periodizations, and event classifications across all modules.

- Roberts, J. M., and Odd Arne Westad. *The Penguin History of the World*. 6th ed. London: Penguin, 2013.
  Narrative context for civilization trajectories, interactions, and causal relationships.

- Bentley, Jerry H., and Herbert F. Ziegler. *Traditions and Encounters: A Global Perspective on the Past*. 6th ed. New York: McGraw-Hill, 2015.
  Cross-cultural perspective on trade networks, cultural diffusion, and regional periodizations.

## Chronology

- Bickerman, Elias J. *Chronology of the Ancient World*. 2nd ed. London: Thames and Hudson, 1980.
  Standard reference for ancient date conventions, astronomical year numbering (negative = BCE), and calendar epoch correlations.

- Grun, Bernard. *The Timetables of History*. 4th ed. New York: Simon and Schuster, 2005.
  Cross-referenced year-by-year chronology for event dating verification.

## Atlases

- Kinder, Hermann, and Werner Hilgemann. *The Penguin Atlas of World History*. 2 vols. London: Penguin, 2003.
  Geographic boundaries, regional assignments, and civilization extents.
