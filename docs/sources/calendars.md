# Calendar Sources

References for calendar system types, epochs, month counts, and leap rules.

## Computational Calendrics

- Dershowitz, Nachum, and Edward M. Reingold. *Calendrical Calculations: The Ultimate Edition*. 4th ed. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2018.
  Definitive reference for all 8 calendar systems: Gregorian, Julian, Islamic (Hijri), Hebrew, Chinese, Hindu Vikram Samvat, Maya Long Count, Egyptian Civil. Epoch correlations, month counts, leap rules, and intercalation algorithms.

## Calendar History

- Richards, E. G. *Mapping Time: The Calendar and Its History*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1998.
  Historical context for calendar adoption, reform, and cultural significance.

## Egyptian Calendar

- Parker, Richard A. *The Calendars of Ancient Egypt*. Chicago: University of Chicago Press, 1950.
  Egyptian Civil calendar as fixed (non-solar) system: 365 days, no leap correction, Sothic cycle drift.
