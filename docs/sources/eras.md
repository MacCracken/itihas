# Era Sources

References for historical period boundaries, categories, and regional periodizations.

## Global Periodization

- Stearns (2001), chapters on periodization and chronological frameworks.
  Source for Bronze Age, Iron Age, Classical Antiquity, Middle Ages, Renaissance, Enlightenment, Industrial Age, Information Age boundaries.

- Bickerman (1980), "Eras and Chronological Systems."
  Astronomical year numbering convention (negative = BCE) used throughout.

## Chinese Dynasties

- Twitchett, Denis, and John K. Fairbank, eds. *The Cambridge History of China*. 15 vols. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1978--2015.
  Authoritative dates for Xia through Qing dynasty boundaries.

## Indian Periods

- Kulke, Hermann, and Dietmar Rothermund. *A History of India*. 6th ed. London: Routledge, 2016.
  Vedic Period, Maurya Period, Gupta Period, Delhi Sultanate, Mughal Period boundaries.

## Mesoamerican Periods

- Coe, Michael D. *Mexico: From the Olmecs to the Aztecs*. 7th ed. London: Thames and Hudson, 2013.
  Preclassic, Classic, Postclassic period boundaries for Mesoamerican civilizations.
