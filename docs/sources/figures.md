# Figure Sources

References for historical figure birth/death dates, civilization associations, and domains.

## Biographical Dictionaries

- Hornblower, Simon, and Antony Spawforth, eds. *The Oxford Classical Dictionary*. 4th ed. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2012.
  Ancient Greek and Roman figures: Hammurabi, Confucius, Aristotle, Julius Caesar, Hannibal.

- Encyclopaedia Britannica, Inc. *Encyclopaedia Britannica*. 15th ed. Chicago: Encyclopaedia Britannica, 2010.
  General biographical reference across all 52 figures.

## Regional

- Twitchett and Fairbank (1978--2015). Chinese figures: Sun Tzu, Confucius, Cai Lun, Seki Takakazu.
- Kulke and Rothermund (2016). South Asian figures: Aryabhata, Guru Nanak, Chandragupta Maurya.
- Levtzion, Nehemia, and Jay Spaulding, eds. *Medieval West Africa: Views from Arab Scholars and Merchants*. Princeton: Markus Wiener, 2003.
  Mansa Musa and West African figures.
