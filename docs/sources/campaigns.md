# Campaign Sources

References for military campaign dates, battles, commanders, belligerents, and outcomes.

## General Military History

- Keegan, John. *A History of Warfare*. New York: Alfred A. Knopf, 1993.
  Campaign classifications, battle analysis methodology, outcome assessments.

- Black, Jeremy. *A Military History of the Western World*. 2 vols. New Haven: Yale University Press, 2009.
  Western campaigns: Greco-Persian Wars through Napoleonic Wars.

- Fuller, J. F. C. *A Military History of the Western World*. 3 vols. New York: Da Capo Press, 1954--1956.
  Battle details, commander assessments, strategic analysis.

## Ancient Campaigns

- Arrian. *The Campaigns of Alexander*. Translated by Aubrey de Selincourt. London: Penguin, 1971.
  Alexander's campaigns: Granicus, Issus, Gaugamela, Hydaspes.

- Goldsworthy, Adrian. *The Fall of Carthage: The Punic Wars 265--146 BC*. London: Cassell, 2000.
  Second Punic War: Cannae, Zama, Hannibal's Alpine crossing.

## Medieval and Early Modern Campaigns

- Nicolle, David. *The Mongol Warlords*. London: Brockhampton Press, 1990.
  Mongol invasion of Khwarezmia: siege dates, campaign timeline.

- Sumption, Jonathan. *The Hundred Years' War*. 5 vols. London: Faber and Faber, 1990--2023.
  Crecy, Agincourt, Orleans: dates, commanders, outcomes.

## Modern Campaigns

- Chandler, David G. *The Campaigns of Napoleon*. New York: Macmillan, 1966.
  Napoleonic Wars: Austerlitz, Trafalgar, Borodino, Waterloo.

- McPherson, James M. *Battle Cry of Freedom: The Civil War Era*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1988.
  American Civil War: Gettysburg, Vicksburg.

- Connaughton, R. M. *The War of the Rising Sun and Tumbling Bear: A Military History of the Russo-Japanese War*. London: Routledge, 1988.
  Russo-Japanese War: Port Arthur, Tsushima.
